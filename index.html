Travel

Flights to France

The closest airports are Geneva, Lyon or Grenoble.

Flights from the UK (London to Geneva/Lyon or Bristol to Geneva) fly regularly with Easyjet and SwissAir. Bristol OR London to Geneva is the most popular and cost efficient. Flights are usually from £30 if booked with plenty of time.

Travel to Tournon

Train from Genève Aéroport to Albertville, 3.20 hours or car, via Annecy, 1.5 hours

Blablacar is a popular way to travel around France for a low-price, either car-sharing or using local buses.

Stay

Albertville

Albertville (a 10 minute drive to Tournon) or around Albertville is the most convenient place to stay.

There are a lot of hotels, gites or Airbnbs around Albertville. Don't hesitate to ask us for advice. Without a car, it's easier to stay in Albertville.

This great nearby eco-gite would be great for a group: It sleeps 15, had 6 rooms and for 3 nights is 1500 Euros (35 euros per night per persons). There is also 3 double rooms in the inn, 125 euros per night for two. If you're interested, let us know and we can arrange a booking. A car is probably best to get there though.

In Tournon, 5min away from the wedding venue, this gite offers many rooms for around 80/90 euros for 2 people per night. It can be found on booking, or you can ask us as well.

If you do stay in Albertville, let us know as we will organise a bus/taxi to pick people up and drive them back after the weddings.
